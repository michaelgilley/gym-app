4-Week Progression (Weeks 7–10)

Week 1 Start compounds at a moderate weight. Build confidence in the new 6–8 rep range.
Week 2 Add 5 lbs to barbell compounds. Progress only if you hit 8 clean reps across all sets.
Week 3 Push intensity. Last compound set should be a genuine grind.
Week 4
DELOAD		 Same exercises, 50–60% of working weights, 2 sets only on everything. Recover and reset.
